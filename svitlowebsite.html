Svitlo-Map

Stay Safe. Find Help. Even Offline.

A humanitarian-first mobile app designed to protect civilians during war and disaster by providing access to verified, life-saving information—even without an internet connection.

Discover How It Works

The Lifeline, Severed.

In conflict zones, infrastructure collapses. Internet access vanishes. Millions are left disconnected from the critical information they need to survive. Existing digital aids fail when they're needed most.

Blackout Failure

Most apps are useless without internet, leaving civilians in an information vacuum during power outages and network cuts.

Unverified Information

Crisis breeds misinformation. Unreliable data on shelters or aid can lead people into greater danger.

Fragmented & Complex

Users need multiple apps for alerts, maps, and aid info. In a crisis, simplicity and an all-in-one solution are vital.

Svitlo-Map: A Lifeline, Restored.

We provide a single, reliable platform that works when nothing else does. We combine offline maps, verified aid data, and danger-aware navigation into one accessible, life-saving tool.

Offline-First Design

Downloadable maps and critical data ensure the app is fully functional without an internet connection.

Verified Aid Directory

Crowdsourced data is verified by NGO partners, providing reliable locations for food, water, shelter, and medicine.

Danger-Aware Routing

GPS-based navigation actively guides users away from reported danger zones to find the safest path.

Emergency SOS

A one-tap SOS button sends location data to NGO responders, with an SMS fallback for when networks are down.

All-in-One Crisis Response

Svitlo-Map integrates essential services into a single, accessible interface, designed for clarity and ease of use in high-stress situations.
